Building & maintaining the database

We need to build a robust, accurate and complete database of all the people involved and keep it up-to-date. We do it using smart technology.

Excel Upload

  • Excel sheets with dispersed profile data are available with college and alumni chapters.
  • Data from multiple sources will be collated by the system, and redundant data will be eliminated to create complete profiles.
  • System will detect syntactical errors in these excel sheets and allow for corrections while uploading them.

Collaborative Editing

  • Allows people to edit other people's profile information, based on privileges.
  • Not all alumni will spend time editing their profile information but this information is useful for other people in the network. For example, an alumnus can add profile information for his batchmates. These edits will then be manually approved by chosen admins from college.
  • This allows complete database to be built quickly.

Data Admins

  • 15 - 20 admins(students+faculty) will be chosen who will spearhead the effort to build and maintain the database.
  • These admins will
    • Add profile information for people of their college.
    • Approve/disapprove edits made to profiles.

Social Network Data

  • Users can choose to link their social media profiles to their profile on Askra.
  • The following are the benefits of doing so
    • Information like education details, employment details etc. will be retrieved automatically and added to the profile in Askra.
    • Changes to profile information will be automatically updated. For example, if you change your job and update the information on LinkedIn, the relevant information will get automatically updated here.

Building a complete alumni database is complicated owing to the large numbers and unavailability of structured data, but with the help of the above technology we can make it happen.


Accurate student, faculty and admin data is available with college and will be regularly updated by the chosen data admins.

Enabling Effective Communication

We strive to increase effectiveness by reducing unwanted communication. The following features of the product help us achieve that.


Search and Filter

Search people based on name, year of graduation, branch, roll number, company, designation, higher education and many more criteria. For example, you can search for all alumni who have graduated from NIT Warangal in 1999 and pursued Masters from Stanford University in one search.

Our intelligent widgets help you further filter down results of your search and get to the right people as quickly as possible. Our search bar also suggests what you might be looking for as soon you start typing in.

Smart Data

Our smart data systems decrease redundancy and increase accuracy of data added by users. For example, if you add TCS as your company we know that people who work at Tata Consultancy Services are your colleagues.

While adding information to your profile, we suggest relevant data to make it easier for you. The system gets smarter as more and more information gets added.

Privacy

We understand your profile information is valuable and keep most of it private by default. You can choose who views what part of your profile conveniently. For example, you can choose to make your email ID visible to all alumni but mobile number visible only to your classmates.

All the data in our system is secured using industry standard security protocols just so you can stay assured that nobody without the right authority gets access to you.

Spam Control

Every user on the system will have communication privileges based on his role with the college. For example, an alumni chapter president can email all alumni in his chapter but the same cannot be done by other alumni. Similarly, an alumni can email a group of his batchmates but cannot email a group of alumni from another batch without additional privileges.

These rules can easily be configured to suit the needs. We also allow you to mark emails as spam and have rules to eliminate or decrease emails from the same people.
